Welcome To Green Yatra

A devoted Green Yatra for a Greener and Sustainable World

Green Yatra is a leading environmental NGO in India and a dedicated green yatra (green journey) towards protection, conservation and betterment of Mother Nature and Humanity. Our sole objective is to create a greener and better world for present and future generations. We are working on our various projects to nullify the environmental imbalance caused by us and our society at large.

Green Yatra is a registered NGO with 12 A, 80G, FCRA and UNEP

Miyawaki Forest

Urban Dense Forests In Less Time And Space

We can grow ultra-fast growing dense, native forests in small patches in different places of the city, such as residential housing societies, gardens, industrial areas, corporate and IT parks and MIDC areas. Forests grown under the Miyawaki method grow 10 times faster, 30 times denser and 100 times more biodiverse compared to a monoculture plantation.

DCB Bank Miyawaki Forest at Mulund and Malad, Mumbai

12,000+ saplings with 60+ native species; one largest and one beautiful Miyawaki Urban Forest for amchi Mumbai. Both dense forest will work like a carbon sink and ground water recharge units in the time to come. Thank you DCB bank and CEO Mr. Murali M. Natrajan to support and join us to create Miyawaki forests for a Green and pollution free Mumbai. Special thanks to BMC to avail us Mulund plantation site. We are creating many Miyawaki Forests in many mega cities (Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Noida, Hyderabad, Nagpur Chennai Gurgaon) for a GreenIndia.

Bag for Cause Drive and Awareness at Soham's Tropical Lagoon CHS

Beat Plastic Pollution On the occasion of the 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, We organised Bag for Cause waste exchange drive and awareness program at Soham's Tropical Lagoon housing Society Thane. Under Bag for Cause initiative you can exchange your Plastic, Raddi and E-waste with Cotton Bags.

Covestro Livelihood Fruits Tree Plantation

Livelihood Plantation to Support Farmers in Palghar District. Thank you #Covestro for supporting 3000+ Fruits Saplings to #SupportFarmers and Create #GreenCover around the Mumbai and #SaveEnvironment We are thankful to our expertise partner Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Dahanu for their valuable support to execute this project.

E-Waste Awareness Session at Mithibai College

Thank you Mithibai College and SVKM for inviting us again for an interactive E-Waste Awareness Session. It is always a pleasure to interact with the youth about important issues concerning our environment because they are the future. Our youth team member, Niksa, spoke about E-Waste – where it comes from, how it is harmful to our environment if not disposed properly, and most importantly, what you can do. We continue to work towards raising awareness for a greener and better India.

GREEN Valentine’s Day Plantation @ Panvel

Green Valentine’s day Celebration - Planting Native fruit and Medicinal plants like Neen, Coconut, etc at “Panvel Municipal Girls School no 3” - Panvel is celebrating Green Valentine’s day today with support from AIESEC MUMBAI and Mission Green Mumbai. Thank you Scott da Silva from Australia and Seiichi Koike from Japan for taking this difficult task of executing. Thank You Priti Goel a true environment lover for taking this project to support schools with Plantation.

Tree Plantation @ Naval Hospital Kanjurmarg

Mission to plant 100,000 native trees across Mumbai in monsoon season 2018, started today at Naval Hospital Kanjurmarg (W). We are starting this year's plantation season with plantation at Naval Hospital Kanjurmarg, NCH Colony, Kanjurmarg (west) Mumbai 400078. We are thankful to the Indian Navy to be part of Green Yatra and Mission GREEN Mumbai. The plantation is supported by AddNode India Pvt. Ltd.
