GREEN India is a leading environmental NGO in India and a dedicated green INDIA towards protection, conservation and betterment of Mother Nature and Humanity. Our sole objective is to create a greener and better world for present and future generations. We are working on our various projects to nullify the environmental imbalance caused by us and our society at large.

Green INDIA is a registered NGO with 14 B, 40A, RNAE and UNEP

We invite you to come together with your community (school, college, housing societies etc.) for the betterment of the environment. It is a step towards creating a big change in society.
The motive of this initiative is to bring residents of a housing society under one umbrella for a green cause and make them understand the importance of going green in our daily life. We organise various social and environmental activities such as:
  • Tree plantations
  • Society clean up drives
  • Old household stuff collection drive
  • Presentations and workshops for all age groups
